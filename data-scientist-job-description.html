Job Description

Lead the development of algorithmic trading strategies for a hedge fund. Backtest proposed trading strategies. Prepare statistical analyses of trading characteristics of various stocks, futures and options. Present the results of each backtested strategy to management.

Responsibilities and Duties:

  • Translate trading ideas into backtestable trading strategies
  • Analyze market behavior
  • Your primary responsibility will be backtesting trading strategies using the library of your choice (ex. BackTrader).
  • Perform and present statistical analyses of trading aspects of different securities

Qualifications:

  • Undergraduate degree is preferred but not required
  • No finance experience is required, as long as you have a curiosity about the work we are doing
  • Python is strongly preferred for both statistical analysis and backtesting
  • This job is fully remote so the ability to work independently is required
